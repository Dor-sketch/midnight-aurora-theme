About Midnight Aurora

Immerse yourself in the ethereal beauty of the northern lights with Midnight Aurora. This sleek, dark theme transforms your browser with deep blues and vibrant teals, mimicking the dance of aurora borealis across a starry night sky. Experience a visually stunning and eye-friendly browsing environment.

Perfect for night owls, astronomy enthusiasts, and anyone who appreciates a calming, beautiful browsing experience.
